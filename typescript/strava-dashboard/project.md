A private dashboard for visualizing Strava training data. Connects via OAuth to the Strava API and renders activity history with charts built in Recharts.

Integrates with Google Gemini to generate AI commentary on recent runs — pacing trends, effort levels, and training notes. Secured with WebAuthn passkey authentication. Deployed as a PWA.
