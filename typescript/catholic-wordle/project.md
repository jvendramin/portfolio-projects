A faith-themed spin on the classic Wordle format. Players guess a daily 5-letter word pulled from Catholic vocabulary — saints' names, theological terms, and liturgical words.

Built as a Progressive Web App with Next.js, installable on mobile. Includes streak tracking, share functionality, and daily word rotation. Word list curated from Catholic tradition.
